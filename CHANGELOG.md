# Changelog

All notable changes to the Rarefied Earth playbook are documented here.

The format is loosely based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/), simplified for a small curated repository. Versions are documentation versions, not semantic software versions; bumps reflect when meaningful new content lands.

## v0.2 — 2026-04-29

### Added

- `agent-operating-system/Brand_Identity.md` — public version of the firm's brand identity specification. Includes positioning thesis, locked tagline, locked Engineering-Editorial brand direction, complete PMS color palette with verified hex values, typography stack, logo philosophy, voice and tone summary. Excludes internal entity details (EIN, registered address, banking, insurance carrier), internal file paths, and contact phone numbers.
- `agent-operating-system/Founder.md` — public-version professional profile for the firm's founder. Includes full career arc, education, peer-reviewed publication, distinctive technical signature, certifications, technical stack, short-form bios. Excludes contact phone, personal email, and license-specific identifiers.
- `CHANGELOG.md` — this file.

### Changed

- Nothing changed in v0.1 files.

### Notes

- Two additional candidates were considered for v0.2 (`Domain_Glossary.md` and `Cohesive_Branding_Research.md`) and explicitly deferred. Both contain enough internal-strategy content that line-by-line sanitization wouldn't produce a useful public artifact. They may return as full rewrites in a future version.

## v0.1 — 2026-04-29

### Added

- Initial public publication of the Rarefied Earth playbook.
- `README.md` — repository overview, what's in here, what's intentionally not in here, license notes.
- `LICENSE` — Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) for textual content. Brand visual assets (logos, color files, typography licenses) remain proprietary trademarks of Earth Evocation Incorporated and are not in this repository.
- `agent-operating-system/Agent_Charter.md` — how to think and behave as a Rarefied Earth operative. Identity, five operating principles, decision-authority model, quality bar, internal vs. external posture, conflict-resolution hierarchy, "true ninja" qualities. Sanitized for public publication.
- `agent-operating-system/Voice_Guide.md` — how to draft on the firm's behalf. The single calibration rule, the hard never's, length and structure heuristics, before/after examples drawn from real drafting work (anonymized). Sanitized for public publication.

### Notes

- This is a curated public subset of the firm's internal operating documents, not a full mirror. Active client engagement state, financial records, internal architecture, brand visual assets, founder personal contact information, and signed contract templates remain in the firm's private workspace.
- The repository is the source of truth for the documents it contains. Internal iCloud working copies are kept in sync via a maintenance script that includes sensitivity-pattern checks before any commit.
